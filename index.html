我們的願景Our Vision

香港是個極度高密度且高壓的城市，對於承受著 PTSD 或性侵害創傷的女孩來說，狹小的城市空間與擠迫的人流，往往織成了一座無法外出的隱形牢籠。

As a city of immense density and societal pressure, Hong Kong's cramped living space and the hustle and bustle becomes an invisible cage for female survivors of trauma and sexual assault.

在這裏，你不需要説話 and 社交，亦不需要出門。我們用一條毛線、一根鈎針，在數位世界為妳隔開外界的雜音，搭建一個安靜虛擬避風港。

We don't force you to speak, nor do we ask you to leave your comfort zone. Through the rhythmic movement of yarn, we isolate the noise of the outside world, creating a 1-square-meter sanctuary in the digital realm.

為什麼妳應該嘗試編織？

如果此時此刻，妳正因為內心一直勇敢對抗的創傷而感到不安與焦慮，編織或許能成為妳心靈的一處溫柔避風港。鈎針一上一下重複且規律的手部動作、毛綫的觸感，就像是一場無聲的正念著陸 (grounding) 練習，能悄悄撫平妳混亂的思緒，讓大腦重新安靜下來。

除了帶來平靜，當妳親眼看著一件精緻的成品在自己手中一點一滴誕生，那種自豪感是無可替代的。這不單單是一門手作，更是一場幫助妳重新建立自信、溫柔地將焦點從外界雜音移開，並找回內心平靜的療癒旅程。

Why should you try crocheting?

If you are feeling unsafe or anxious from the invisible trauma you have been bravely fighting, crocheting can be a gentle sanctuary for your mind. The soft, repetitive hand movements and the touch of the yarn can act as a natural grounding exercise, helping to soothe your thoughts and quiet the chaos.

Beyond the calm, imagine the pride of looking at a beautiful piece made entirely by your own hands. It is more than just a craft—it is an empowering journey to rebuild your confidence, gently shifting your focus away from the noise and back to your own inner peace.
