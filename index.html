我們的看見 / Our Vision

香港是個極度高密度且高壓的城市。對於承受著 PTSD 或性侵害創傷的女孩來說，狹小的居住空間與社會對心理健康的污名化，往往織成了一座無法外出的隱形牢籠。

Hong Kong is a city of immense density and pressure. For survivors of trauma and sexual assault, cramped living spaces and mental health stigma often form an invisible cage, making social interaction feel impossible.

我們不強迫妳說話，也不要求妳出門。我們用一條毛線、一根鈎針，在數位世界為妳隔開外界的雜音，搭建一個一平方公尺的虛擬避風港。

We don't force you to speak, nor do we ask you to leave your comfort zone. Through the rhythmic movement of yarn, we isolate the noise of the outside world, creating a 1-square-meter sanctuary in the digital realm.

療癒項目 / The Ritual

01. 居家材料包 / Sensory Kits

免費且完全保密地為妳寄送客製化的「感官療癒毛線包」與工具，從零基礎教學影片開始，順應妳自己的節奏。

Complimentary and strictly confidential healing yarn kits delivered to your doorstep, complete with gentle, self-paced tutorials.

02. 遠端靜音織間 / Silent Studio

不開鏡頭、預設靜音的線上陪伴空間。大家只是在溫柔的低頻音樂中，看著彼此專注編織的手部動作，感受無聲的凝聚力。

Camera-off, muted virtual sessions. We gather in silence under calming ambient music, sharing solidarity through the non-verbal craft of hands.

安全與邊界 / Boundaries

【核心安全宣言】 本組織嚴格限制僅限女性、跨性別及非二元性別等遭受性別暴力的倖存者參與。進入核心社群前均需通過充滿同理心的背景審查，以確保社群具備 100% 的同質性與安全邊界。

To protect the psychological safety of our members, this space is strictly reserved for women, trans, and non-binary survivors of gender-based violence. An empathetic background verification is required to maintain a 100% secure, trigger-free boundary.
